2. Setup & Authentication

1. First-Run & Auto-Seeding​

When you install Markd and open it for the very first time, it scans your active Chrome bookmark root (usually Other Bookmarks).

Markd will instantly load its Default Starter Template. You will see categories like Technology, Finance, and Entertainment appear in Plan (Blue text/badge). Because they are Blue, they haven't touched your Chrome bookmarks yet. You can review them, and if you like them, click Apply Sync Plan to physically create them.

note

If your active bookmark root is not empty, Markd will automatically detect your existing folders and notify you via a toast message — for example: "48 new folders found. Click 'Clean & Resync Plan' to add them."

2. New Device Setup Wizard​

If you install Markd on a browser that already has custom folders, the app will not overwrite them blindly. Instead, it triggers the New Device Setup wizard.

You will be presented with a warning: "The app has loaded its default folder template, but your browser already has custom folders."

  • Import My Browser Structure: This instantly wipes the default Markd template, scans your Chrome bookmarks, and builds a Blueprint that perfectly matches what you already have.
  • Start with Default Template: This ignores your browser's current folders and keeps the default Markd template active.
note

If you choose Start with Default Template, your existing browser folders will show up as "Out of Sync" later.

3. Authentication & Sessions​

Markd requires you to sign in with your Google Account. We use this simply to link your browser securely to your Free or Premium subscription status without making you remember a separate password.

Why does Markd require an active internet connection? Beyond verifying your subscription tier, Markd relies on the internet to perform its core tasks:

  • AI Processing: To securely communicate with cloud-based AI providers like Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google.
  • Reading Websites: When you ask Markd to sort a bookmark, its internal scraper must briefly visit that website to read its content.
  • Link Checking: The "Check Broken Links" tool needs the internet to ping your saved websites and ensure they are still alive.
note

If you are a Premium user using the local 'Ollama' AI provider, your AI prompts remain entirely on your machine, but Markd still requires an internet connection to read the websites and verify your session.

4. Offline Mode & Lockdown​

Markd is designed to fail safely if your internet connection drops or if its background verification check fails to reach the server.

  • Instant Detection: If your Wi-Fi drops while you are working, or if the 2-hour server check fails to reach the internet, Markd instantly drops into Offline Lockdown mode.
  • What happens in Lockdown?
    • The sidebar status changes to a warning: "Offline".
    • An orange banner appears at the bottom left reading "Connection Lost: AI & Sync unavailable" (or "Pro features paused" for Premium users).
    • All AI-dependent buttons (like Sort Creating New, Check Broken Links, and Preview) are visually disabled and their tooltips change to "Feature paused while offline."
    • The AI Provider settings tab is completely greyed out and unclickable.
  • Recovery: Once your connection returns, simply click the Reconnect / Verify button in the orange sidebar banner. Markd will ping the server, restore your session, and gracefully re-enable all buttons and animations without requiring a page reload.
